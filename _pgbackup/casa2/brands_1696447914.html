Our Mezcal brands are in the Top 10 of the best-selling and most recognized brands in Mexico.

They participate in the three most profitable price segments of the industry.

They cover the organoleptic profiles for all types of mezcal consumers: beginners, enthusiasts, and experts.

Our latest brand, Metchi®, is a premium artisanal worm salt, ideal for accompanying mezcal, either straight with orange slices or in mixology.

Directly from the ancestral capital of mezcal, EL RECUERDO MEZCAL® is a memorable product of our tradition and culture, in every sense of the word.

EL RECUERDO MEZCAL® is offered with an agave worm, for those who appreciate the silky texture that it brings to the liquid and enjoy the novelty of sharing it with friends. It is also available without the worm for those seeking smoky notes with a different complexity.

Baked on river stones with certified wood, all traditional techniques are respected, guaranteeing the preservation of the environment.

EL RECUERDO MEZCAL® is the best-selling mezcal in Duty Free Mexico stores; it is chosen by international tourists looking for authentic gifts from Mexico.

ZIGNUM ®, means "spearhead", is recognized as an exceptionally smooth and fine mezcal.

The Master Mezcalero and his people make it with passion and commitment to the environment; from the field to the barrel and from the barrel to the bottle. The agave pineapples are harvested at their peak of ripeness, to give the final distillate an authentic flavor.

ZIGNUM ® Reposado, Añejo and Cristalino are then aged in American white oak barrels, which give them greater complexity and character.

THE MOST AWARDED MEZCAL IN THE WORLD, will please the most demanding customers, as well as those who are starting to venture into the world of mezcal.

SEÑORÍO MEZCAL® is the Perfect Harmony of two distillations

SEÑORÍO MEZCAL® is a Soft Smoked Mezcal originating from Oaxaca, which acquires this particularity thanks to the PERFECT HARMONY OF TWO DISTILLATIONS of different intensities and flavor-aroma profiles.

THE PERFECT BALANCE BETWEEN SMOOTH SMOKE NOTES AND THE AUTHENTIC FLAVOR OF ESPADÍN AGAVE. SEÑORÍO MEZCAL® Reposado and Añejo are matured in American white oak barrels, which give them more intense and complex notes.

We celebrate and invite discovery, in Perfect Harmony, of the value of small details and learning to enjoy the "whole" of things.

METCHI® balances and complements the mezcal ritual

Metchi® is the abbreviation for (metchikuil), a traditional way of naming the red maguey worm in Zapotec (an indigenous language of the Oaxacan region).

Our worm salt is a smoky and spicy mix with a pleasant presence of earthy flavors.

Metchi® balances and complements the mezcal ritual, it is ideal for frosting your cocktails and a delicious seasoning for Mexican and international dishes.
