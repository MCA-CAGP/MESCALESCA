Don Antonio Rivera Venegas Foundation

El Agave y Nosotros Foundation aims to:

 

· Contribute to the enrichment of artistic and cultural activity through the preservation and dissemination of the artistic heritage of Oaxaca.

 

· We work with the artistic community to promote respect for traditions, generating an ethical and social commitment with the support of artisans in the community.

 

· Show one of the most extensive and representative collections of Oaxacan art, thus promoting respect for plurality and contributing to the development of the State.
