ZIGNUM, which means “tip of the spear”, is celebrated as an exceptionally smooth,

refined Mezcal.

 

The Maestro Mezcalero & his team, work with an eco-friendly mindset from field to barrel to bottle. We harvest the agave hearts (piña) at the peak of maturity, with a focus on bringing out the authentic flavor of the agave in the finished spirit.

 

ZIGNUM Reposado & Añejo are then aged in oak barrels for added complexity and character.

Straight out the ancestral capital of mezcal, EL RECUERDO MEZCAL is a memorable product of our tradition and our culture, in every sense of the word.

 

EL RECUERDO MEZCAL is available with a worm for those who prefer the silky texture the agave-worm gives the liquid and the novelty of sharing it with friends, as well as without the worm for those who seek smoky notes with a different complexity.

 

Cooked with certified wood over river rocks, the traditional techniques are honored and preserve the environment as well.

 

EL RECUERDO MEZCAL is the number one Mezcal sold in Duty Free; it is selected by international tourists seeking authentic gifts from Mexico.

Metchi da balance y complementa el ritual mezcalero

 

Respetando la tradición que nuestros ancestros nos enseñaron. Desde la recolección del gusano, que se hace solo una vez al año en temporada de lluvias, buscando en cada maguey los mejores gusanos.

 

Metchi es la abreviación para “metchikuil”, una forma tradicional de nombrar al gusano rojo del maguey en zapoteco (lenguaje autóctono de la región oaxaqueña).

 

Nuestra mezcla es ahumada y picosita con agradable presencia de sabores de la tierra.
