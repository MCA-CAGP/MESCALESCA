Learn about Mezcal on our Tour where you will live a unique experience, knowing the process of making our mezcal, from the field to the bottle.

MORE INFORMATION

On the Tour you can:

 

• Sow your own agave seed and be part of the growth of our fields.

• Visit our Barrel Room.

• And, of course, try our Mezcal.

In addition, you will learn about the SUSTAINABILITY PROGRAM MÉTODO VERDE® that our distillery has, unique in the region.


At the end of the Tour you will also be able to meet and acquire handicrafts in the art gallery of the Fundación El Agave y Nosotros, where various pieces made by talented artisans from Oaxaca are exhibited, helping to preserve the identity and heritage of the Oaxacan culture.
